COIDA — Workmen's Compensation

Since the 2020 Constitutional Court ruling, every household employer must register with the Compensation Fund. If your worker is injured and you're not registered, you pay personally — uncapped. Annual assessment costs as little as R164/year. DomestiSure tracks your deadline and calculates your assessment automatically.
